Create your account

You need an account before you can send any forms to the registry.

1. Tap CREATE ACCOUNT in the menu
2. Fill in your name, email, and address
3. Upload a photo of your ID
4. Submit and wait for approval
⏳
Wait for approval
The Ministry (MTCIC) will check your ID and approve your account. You will receive an email when it is ready.
[I-Kiribati: Tataninga approval mai MTCIC]
💡 Tip: You can take a photo of your ID directly from your phone camera when uploading.
business.gov.kiLOG IN

Get permission to re-register

You need official permission (called "authority") to update your business name on the registry.

1. Log in to your account
2. Search for your business name
3. Tap on your business name
4. Tap REQUEST ENTITY AUTHORITY
5. Upload your ID or letter of authority, then submit

Wait for approval
The Ministry will check your request. You will receive an email when your permission is approved.
